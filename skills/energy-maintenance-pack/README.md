# Energy Maintenance Intelligence Pack

A domain-specific AI skill pack designed for renewable energy asset management and condition-based maintenance.

## Current Modules

### Gearbox Intelligence (v1.0.0)

Evaluates wind turbine gearbox condition using:

- Visual inspection findings (borescope)
- Oil iron (Fe ppm) analysis
- Vibration status
- Temperature anomalies
